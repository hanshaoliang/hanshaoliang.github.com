SKT Lab is a global software product development company and incubator. Our focus is on core software development with the world's top engineering talent at your fingertips. We help our clients tackle the complexities of building commercial grade software and help build products and take them to market faster. Our relentless focus on quality, efficiency, and customer service has resulted in the development of many successful products for our customers and high client satisfaction. By strategically balancing the advantages of our unique development teams with the unique needs of each client, SKT Lab delivers superior software solutions allowing its customers to compete in today's global economy.

For companies and entrepreneurs with ideas or brand new products, we are also a product incubator offering product development, fund raising, and commercialization services. We will help you nurture your product idea, develop it and launch it worldwide. Our offices are in the USA and China, allowing for a global perspective and global reach. Have a great product idea? Allow us to bring your idea to life.
