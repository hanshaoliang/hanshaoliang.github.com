Systems Engineer, Cupertino, CA

    Responsibilities:
  • Scaling to meet the demands of a data-hungry internet and defending our infrastructure from security threats.
  • Develop, and drive innovative solutions to automate our day-to-day work.
  • Contribute to and maintain system standards.
  • Wrangling our servers to keep them healthy, happy and hard-working.
  • Supporting engineering teams across the company to ensure a reliable platform upon which they can build their systems.

    Qualifications:
  • Experience running and maintaining a 24x7 Internet-based service or product.
  • Detailed knowledge of and experience with Linux / RedHat + RPMs and yum.
  • Evidence of successfully designing and/or implementing system automation (i.e. performance monitoring, configuration management, etc.) for use in a 24x7 environment.
  • A solid grasp of network fundamentals.
  • Demonstrable Python or Perl expertise.
  • B.A or B.S. degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
  • 5-7 years in a Systems Administration or operations role.
  • Strong communication skills: able to clearly articulate, defend and document design decisions and work with a distributed team.
    • Ideal candidates will also have the following:
  • Experience with very large, high-throughput distributed systems.
  • General knowledge of Internet and operating system security fundamentals.
  • Familiarity with Amazon AWS (S3 & EC2).
  • Experience with MySQL administration and/or Amazon RDS.
  • Hands-on experience with monitoring tools, such as Nagios, Zabbix or CloudWatch.
  • An expert understanding of automation technologies such as puppet, cuisine and fabric.
