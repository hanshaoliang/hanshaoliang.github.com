Senior Engagement Marketing Manager

  • Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Marketing Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Experienced
  • Role
    • This team manager is responsible for maturing our customer relationships through coordinated, customized and segmented messaging in social media, email, in-application and other similar channels. The right individual will have experience defining and driving successful engagement marketing strategies and tactics for B2C companies.
  • Responsibilities
  • Owner of UBERTAL customer communications, to ensure customers are receiving pertinent, well-coordinated and timely communications. This role will own metrics such as retention, communication engagement, count of brands owned, cross-product engagement.
  • Define customer segments and a systematic communication strategy to effectively reach and mature our audience.
  • Work closely with Product Management, Creative and Web Development to create, test and refine communications channels for improved success (e.g. in-application messaging, Facebook Connect).
  • Internal owner of communication and monitoring toolset (e.g. Hubspot, Radian6).
  • Report program successes, opportunities, improvements and testing.
  • Manage and develop team of two direct reports. Own engagement project and project timelines.
  • Requirements
  • 7-10 years of engagement, CRM, retention and/or email marketing experience.
  • Bachelors’ degree. Masters’ degree a plus.
  • Excellent project management skills with flexibility to juggle multiple priorities.
  • Experience developing, delivering and measuring B2C marketing initiatives.
  • Demonstrated ability to develop strategic marketing plans and oversee their implementation.
  • Motivated self-starter with good interpersonal skills.
  • Detail-oriented individual with excellent management and organizational skills.
  • Start-up experience is a plus.
