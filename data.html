Javascript Platform Engineer, Cupertino, CA

    Responsibilities:
  • Working with the product and engineering teams to design and develop our mobile platform.
  • Document software design and be able to communicate decisions with team members.
  • Build SDKs for major mobile platforms.
  • Develop unit and integration tests.
  • Deploy software in operational settings.
  • Handle operational issues that arise within a software platform with uptime requirements.

    Qualifications:
  • Someone who has at least 5 years of experience with Javascript.
  • Someone exposed to iOS and Android developer platforms.
  • Someone with strong communication skills to work with customers and a distributed team.
  • Someone who can deploy software in operational settings and handle issues that come with a system that has uptime requirements.
  • Someone with interest in the latest web developments, whether its browsers, languages or tools.
  • Someone who understands Scrum/Agile methodologies.
