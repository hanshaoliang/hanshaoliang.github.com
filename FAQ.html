FAQ

  • What does Ubertal do?
    • We specialize in finding top tier engineers from around the globe including China, India, Eastern Europe, Mexico and the US. We help growing US companies find top tier talent for contract work and permanent positions. Engineers may be remote or onsite. We often help the candidates come to the US to work at our client site by sponsoring their H1B visa.
  • What are the major advantages for using Ubertal services?
    • • Access to an amazing pool of software development talent from all over the world
    • Pre-screened candidates to help minimize your recruiting cycle. Our engineers are pre-screened using a very stringent process including technical testing, English testing and personality testing. This helps eliminate the noise of having to sift through hundreds of resumes to find one great one.
    • We offer two week risk free trial of our engineers. If you are not satisfied, you do not have to pay.
  • Are there any upfront costs in working with you?
    • No. We do not charge any upfront fees. You pay for your engineers after they begin working.
  • What criteria do you use to qualify engineers?
    • We follow the below criteria when qualifying our engineers for work with Ubertal:
    1.Graduated from top 10 engineering schools
    2.Minimum 3 years experience working for a US based company
    3.Great English (written and verbal)
    4.Pass our rigorous coding test
    5.Pass our rigorous personality test
  • What kind of engagement models do you have?
    • • Permanent hire: In this case we charge a percentage of the engineer’s first year salary as recruiting fee.
    • Contractor: We charge an hour rate to our client and engineer is on Ubertal payroll.
    • Contract to hire: Our engineers work as contractor initially on your project and can be converted to your full time employees after a predefined period of time and predefined conversion fee.
  • Where are Ubertal engineers located?
    • Depending on the engagement model, we have engineers both offshore and onsite. Onsite engineers are typically more expensive than offshore engineers. Some of our clients hire both onsite engineers (as team leader) and offshore engineers (to save cost) to work on their projects.
  • How do I make sure our IP is protected?
    • Ubertal is in the business of staffing good engineers. Any IP related agreement is between you and the engineers we identified for you, just like any contractors or employees you hire by yourself. We are more than happy to facilitate the process if needed.
  • How long would I have to wait to get an engineer for my requirement?
    • Typically it would take anywhere from 1-2 weeks for us to send you software developers that meet your requirements.
  • Do you work on a project basis?
    • At this time, we do not work on a project basis. We may do this in the future.
