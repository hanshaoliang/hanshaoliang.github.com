Product Manager, Broker Dealer

  • Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Product Management Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Mid Level
  • UBERTAL is looking for a Product Manager to drive the evolution and growth of our Broker Dealer platform. The ideal candidate will have launched more than one successful product, and have developed it from inception to release. We’re a consumer web company coupled with a broker dealer. We prefer some experience in a similar environment, including consumer web, mobile, cloud, or possibly enterprise software. We follow agile methodology and would prefer someone who has agile experience.
  • Responsibilities
  • • Help build our Broker Dealer platform from the ground up.
  • • Work with Engineering, Marketing, Legal, Sales, external partners, customers, and the UBERTAL management team to set product development priorities.
  • • Identify/gather product requirements and develop user stories.
  • • Ensure new features are reliable and scalable.
  • • Support cross-functional initiatives.
  • • Participate in the product hiring process.
  • Requirements
  • • Knowledge of financial accounting and legal requirements such as Cost Basis and Tax reporting.
  • • Experience with stock trading platforms or related space.
  • • Product Manager working directly with user experience design and engineering teams.
  • • Passion for conceiving, designing, building, shipping and sustaining amazing products.
  • • BS in a technical or quantitative discipline; MBA a plus.
  • • Experience in managing aggressive and dynamic projects with cross-functional teams and competing priorities.
  • • Business mindset with strong analytical skills.
  • • Build relationships cross-functionally with other product teams, technology, QA and customer service.
  • • Self-starter with the interest, ability and energy to work in a fast-paced start-up environment.
  • • Excellent written and verbal communications skills.
  • • Agile experience a strong plus.
  • REPORTS TO Director, Product Management
  • LOCATION San Francisco’s Financial District
