What We Do

Ubertal is an international talent acquisition and placement company for software developers. Enhance your development team with our engineers. We help cut out the noise when seeking engineering talent. A fresh perspective on recruiting. Access to a new talent pool. We source top engineering talent from around the globe, qualify them through our intensive screening process and place them at our partner companies. We work with only the best and brightest engineers from around the globe, typically those who have multinational work experience, excellent communication skills, strong work ethic and are great team players. The companies we work with are leading companies in the US, seeking only the best talent.
