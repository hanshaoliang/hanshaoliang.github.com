Engagement Model

We have a number of different ways to engage, from contracting to hiring your permanent employees. One of our strengths is our ability to be flexible to your changing business needs. We will work with you to create the model or combination of models that are right for your business.
  • Permanent Hire through Visa Sponsorship
  • - In this model, we identify and qualify candidates from the global talent pool. You interview them and decide whether you would like to sponsor their visa. Before October 1st, you have the option to have the engineer work remotely on a contract basis. The engineer can also come onsite for training during this time. You can evaluate the engineer prior to April 1st after which you would apply for their visa if you decide to hire him/her.
  • Onsite Contractor/Contract to hire
  • - In this model, we sponsor a candidate’s visa and they could be your onsite contractor. You have the option to convert them to full time employees or keep them as contractors.
  • Remote Contractor
  • - Engineers work from home and you benefit from the cost savings of offshore development. The engineer can come periodically onsite for training purposes. The engineer may even work during US working hours if required.
  • Permanent hire via US talent pool
  • - We will help you source the best engineering talent that already have work visas to work in the US. Our process for finding candidates and qualifying them is extremely rigorous. Our focus is on quality and simplification of the recruiting process.
