The Process

At Ubertal, we believe that our success lies in our ability to truly understand your requirements, not just on a cursory level but in depth. This understanding gives us the ability to find just the right candidate for your needs. We also believe that your time is valuable and our candidate pre-qualification process ensures that only the best candidates are working with you. Here’s a little bit about our process. It’s very simple:
  • 1.Tell us what your needs are
    • Are you looking for permanent employees or contractors?
    What types of technology skills are you looking for?
    How many years of experience do you need?
  • 2.We find you candidates that match your requirements
    • - Within a few days, we will provide you with candidate profiles to meet your needs
    - You can select the candidates you think would be a fit and run them through your interview process
    - Once a decision is made, the engineers are ready to work on your project
  • 3.Work with us RISK FREE
    • We allow you to work with our engineers on a risk free basis so that you can decide if they are a good fit for your requirements. No hassle. No payment. If you are not satisfied with the engineer, we will find the right person for you.
