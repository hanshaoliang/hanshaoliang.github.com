The Process

  • 1.Tell us what your needs are
    • a.Are you looking for permanent employees or contractors?
    b.What types of technology skills are you looking for?
    c.How many years of experience?
  • 2.We will find you candidates that match your requirements
    • a.Within a few days, we will provide you with candidate profiles to meet your needs
    b.You can select the candidates you think would be a fit and run them through your interview process

    • a.We allow you to work with our engineers on a risk free basis so that you can decide if they are a good fit for your requirements. No hassle. No payment. If you are not satisfied with the engineer, we will find the right person for you.
