Frequently Asked Questions

How can we get funding for our startup?
Email us at idea@sktlab.com with your idea described in 100 words or less.

How much do you invest?
There are a couple of ways that we typically invest:

We contribute engineering resources (1-5 engineers) for 1-3 months on prototyping or release 1 of the product.
We may also invest anywhere from $10,000-$50,000 USD to help establish the initial customer base.

What types of companies do you invest in and in what geographies?
We fund technology companies. Typically these include mobile or cloud based software companies. We're also looking for companies that can have a global reach. Our focus is on companies targeting markets in the US, China and India.

What does SKT Lab get out of this?
We offer various partnership options. These could include equity or other collaboration models such as revenue sharing.

Do we need to write a business plan?
No. We will contact you to set up the necessary steps which include an application and interviews.

I have a great idea but most 5th graders can code better than me. Will you help me find the technical team to develop the idea?
Yes. In many cases we provide an engineering team to help develop the idea.

Do you help find additional investors?
Yes. Our objective is to help an early stage company prepare itself for the next round of funding.
