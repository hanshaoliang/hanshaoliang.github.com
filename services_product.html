Frequently Asked Questions
Read on for Frequently Asked Questions and to learn more about our requirements and how you can apply

How can we get funding for our startup?
Email us at idea@sktlab.com with your idea and why you want to partner with us.

How much do you invest?
There are a couple of ways for us to invest:
We contribute engineering resources (1-5 engineers) for 1-3 months on prototyping or release 1 product.
We may also invest anywhere from $10,000-$50,000 USD to help establish the initial customer base.

What types of companies do you invest in and in what geographies?

What does SKT Lab get out of this?

Do we need to write a business plan?
No. We will contact you to set up the necessary steps which include an application and interviews.

Yes. In many cases we provide an engineering team to help develop the idea.

Do you help find additional investors?
Yes. Our objective is to help an early stage company prepare itself for the next round of funding.
