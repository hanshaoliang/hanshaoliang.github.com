Management

Pavan Kochar

Co-Founder and CEO

Pavan possesses a breadth of experience from global consulting, with an emphasis on building software engineering teams in China and India, to founding companies. Prior to SKT Lab, Pavan held leadership positions at a number of global technology consulting services firms, including VanceInfo (now Pactera), Infosys, Accenture and Deloitte Consulting. As Senior Principal, Innovative Solutions at Infosys Consulting, Pavan pioneered the integration of Infosys's BPO, Technologies and Consulting services into a unified offering for Cisco. At Valdero, a Kleiner Perkins-funded supply chain startup, Pavan was vital to both product development and client acquisition.

Pavan founded the Dallas-Fort Worth chapter of TIE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) as well as Grapevine, a startup forum in the pre-accelerator era. She was also a Board Member at Indus Womens Leaders.

Pavan holds a B.S in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where she was honored as a Knight of St. Pat.

Hue Wang

Co-Founder and CTO

Hue has Over 20 years of experience in architecture, design, and development of server, desktop and mobile software systems. He held senior technical leadership positions at a number of world famous software and internet companies.

Hue has a M.S. degree in Computing and Information Sciences from Rochester Institute of Technology, and Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University.

Bowen Wang

Advisor

Bowen is an innovative product leader and entrepreneur with 15+ years’ experience in enterprise software, with a focus in eGovernment and smart city solutions. He was the senior vice president at Accela overseeing the engineering department of the industry’s leading software vendor for government licensing, permitting, and asset management system, with over 500 federal, state, and local government customers worldwide. He brings a unique combination of strong technical background, in-depth understanding of product management and business development, and first-hand experience with enterprise government software solutions. Prior to joining Accela, Mr. Wang directed architecture and system integration at Capgemini Government Solutions for a major software project at the State of Nevada. Before that, Mr. Wang served as the product strategy director for public sector industry at Oracle Corporation, managing CRM product releases and go-to-market strategy. Earlier, Mr. Wang was an engineering lead at a number of software companies, including Siebel Systems, Syncsort Incorporated, and Bristol Technologies.

Mr. Wang graduated from the University of California, Berkeley, with an M.B.A. He also holds a Master of Science in computer science from Ohio University, and a Bachelor of Science in materials engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in China.
