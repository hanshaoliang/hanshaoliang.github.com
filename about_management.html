Vince Xu
Co-Founder and CEO
As CEO and Co-Founder of SKT Lab, Vince oversees the company's direction and brings over 20 years of experience in global consulting and product development. Vince has been an active angel investor for many years, specifically focused on the software industry. He was a solution principal at Infosys Consulting, running multiple solution offerings within high tech vertical. Prior to Infosys, Vince was in software product management space managing software development for Oracle, Selectica, and BEA.

Vince is an active member of the Kerietsu Forum, the largest angel investment community in the world.
Vince has a B.S. in Computer Science from Sichuan University, China, and MBA from Yale University, USA.
