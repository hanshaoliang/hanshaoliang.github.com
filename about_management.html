Vince Xu
Co-Founder and CEO
As CEO and Co-Founder of SKT Lab, Vince oversees the company's direction and brings over 20 years of experience in global consulting and product development. Vince has been an active angel investor for many years, specifically focused on the software industry. He was a solution principal at Infosys Consulting, running multiple software solution offerings within the high tech vertical. Prior to Infosys, Vince was in the software product management space managing software development for Oracle, Selectica, and BEA.

Vince is an advisor and board member of a number of startups in Silicon Valley. Vince is an active member of the Kerietsu Forum, the largest angel investment community in the world.

Vince has a B.S. in Computer Science from Sichuan University, China, and MBA from Yale University.
Hue Wang

Hue has Over 20 years of experience in architecture, design, and development of server, desktop and mobile software systems. He held senior technical leadership positions at a number of world famous software and internet companies.

Hue has a M.S. degree in Computing and Information Sciences from Rochester Institute of Technology, and Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University.
Pavan Kochar
Co-Founder and EVP Business Development
Pavan oversees client and partner acquisition at SKT Lab and possesses a breadth of experience from global consulting, with an emphasis on building software engineering teams in China and India, to founding companies. Prior to SKT Lab, Pavan held leadership positions at a number of global technology consulting services firms, including VanceInfo (now Pactera), Infosys, Accenture and Deloitte Consulting. As Senior Principal, Innovative Solutions at Infosys Consulting, Pavan pioneered the integration of Infosys's BPO, Technologies and Consulting services into a unified offering for Cisco. At Valdero, a Kleiner Perkins-funded supply chain startup, Pavan was vital to both product development and client acquisition.

Pavan founded the Dallas-Fort Worth chapter of TIE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) as well as Grapevine, a startup forum in the pre-accelerator era. She was also a Board Member at Indus Womens Leaders.

Pavan holds a B.S in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where she was honored as a Knight of St. Pat.
Doug McGraw
VP Sales
Doug brings to SKTLabs more than 20 years of sales experience, with an emphasis in Enterprise Software, IT Outsourcing and Consulting Services. Having specialized in sales for both software and services in the high tech industry vertical, Doug is well versed in the areas of software product development and product engineering managed services.

As Vice President of Sales, Doug motivates the company's sales team and drives revenue through large scale, strategic partnerships.

Over the last two decades, Doug has held senior sales positions at Hewlett-Packard, Infosys Technologies, i2 Technologies, SAP and Oracle.

Doug holds a B.S. in Business from the State University of New York, College at Brockport.
