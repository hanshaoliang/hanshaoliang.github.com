Sr. QA Engineer

Location: San Francisco, CA Department: Engineering Min. Experience: Experienced

Company QA Team is a dynamic, experienced team responsible for testing of product. As a member of the QA group, you will work closely with product management and developers to ensure high quality and timely product releases.
We’re an agile environment and our tech stack is Scala - Java - Redis - Mongo DB - Ruby.

Responsibilities

• Participate in product feature design and discussions , by working closely with product and developers.

• Conduct reviews of requirements specifications .

• Participate in testing, test planning , including identifying unit, system, end to end, and regression testing.

• Support existing test automation  efforts.

• Evaluate quality risk for product changes/fixes and propose test plans to address risks.

• Participate in weekly deployments on required basis.

Requirements

• Experience with testing web and mobile applications.

• Experience testing large scale systems.

• Experience working with databases: MySQL, SQL Server or other database management servers.

• Experience working with Linux.

• Experience with SaaS and cloud software environment is a big plus.

• Understanding of web technologies - browsers, web servers, application servers, and http protocol.

• Strong understanding of QA methodologies and processes .

• Strong communication and documentation skills.

• Experience with automation frameworks and tools like Cucumber, JUnit or Selenium is a big plus.

• Knowledge of scripting languages such as Ruby, Python, Shell is a big plus.

• BS/MS in Computer Science (or equivalent work experience).
